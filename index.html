why duologue

Different from the tools you already have.

Duologue isn't list-building, intent data, or a prompt wrapper. It's a dedicated reasoning layer for account intelligence.

vs. Clay / Apollo

List-building is step one. Business cases close deals.

Clay and Apollo are excellent at enrichment and list creation. Duologue picks up after that, constructing business cases and aligning them to each stakeholder in the buying committee.

vs. Bombora / 6sense

Intent tells you when. Duologue tells you what to say.

Intent signals flag accounts in research mode. Duologue provides the substantive context, what to discuss and why it matters to each person when you reach them.

vs. Custom GPT

A persistent system, not a one-shot prompt.

Custom GPTs lack continuous monitoring, structured context tracking, and stakeholder mapping. Duologue is a persistent research system, not enhanced prompting.

vs. RevOps tooling

Data assembly is table stakes. Interpretation is the hard part.

RevOps can assemble data and trigger workflows. But interpreting the growth story for each stakeholder requires a dedicated reasoning layer that standard tools don't provide.
